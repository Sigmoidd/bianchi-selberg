# Track B Humbert partition audit

Status: **FAIL CLOSED**.  This artifact does not change
`rung4_certified=false`.

## What is exact

`track_b_partition_geometry.py` records, with Gaussian-integer matrices and
rational arithmetic, the five codimension-one Humbert face transitions

`T1`, `T1^-1`, `R`, `Ti R`, and `S`,

their six-copy inverse-right-action permutations, the two cusp coordinate
conventions, and exact stabilizers at the six finite special points.
It also records a candidate product of quintic `C2` gates extending the fixed
Track-B cutoff.  The product identity proves coverage on the *closed reference
cell as a set*.

The existing symmetric 24-split mesh is useful only for its exact face tags.
It is an inner polyhedral approximation `K_h subset K`; its curved sliver is
not meshed.  It therefore cannot prove coverage by smooth charts on the true
orbifold.

## Smallest missing lemma

For the explicit gate width `epsilon=1/10`, prove that the thickened true
Humbert cell below `y=6/5` meets exactly the incident chambers generated by
the recorded face cycles, and no other translate.  The proof must enumerate
all edge/vertex chamber-to-chamber words and the complete singular-stratum
incidence inventory.  This is an explicit collar-separation/Lebesgue-number lemma for
the curved Humbert polyhedron, not a numerical mesh-refinement statement.

Until this lemma exists, local finiteness, subordination, the edge/vertex
transition list, and placement of elliptic averaging are not certified.  Hence neither
`b0` nor `b1` is a theorem input.

## Conditional size audit (not a certificate)

Using `max |smoothstep'| <= 15/8`, `max |smoothstep''| <= 6`, the elementary
volume bound `vol(F)<=1/2`, and deliberately coarse product-rule estimates,
the candidate gates would give

    b0 < 54744.97874481373
    b1 <   415.3321935811511

*if* the missing collar lemma held.  Against `R<0.0101903405004245`, an equal
split of the residual budget would require

    delta0 < 9.3071006091037e-8
    delta1 < 1.2267698793777017e-5.

These numbers are diagnostic only and are stored under
`conditional_bounds_not_theorem_inputs`.  The certified top-level `b0_upper`
and `b1_upper` are intentionally `null`.

## Machine interface

`track_b_partition_result.json` is the canonical fail-closed artifact.  Its
top-level strict flags include coverage, local finiteness, transition-set
completeness, stabilizer completeness, two-cusp coordinates, and partition
constants.  `overlap_interface.active_transitions` gives the exact face rows
and GLUE permutations, but consumers must reject it until all strict flags
are true.

## Reproduction

    python -m py_compile track_b_partition_geometry.py
    python track_b_partition_geometry.py --depth 5

The point stabilizers are independently reproduced by a word search.  Their
certified orders are `4,6,6,6,12,12`.  Exhaustiveness does not rely on word
depth: the fixed-point denominator and its inverse bound `|c|^2<=2` and
`|a|,|d|<=2`, after which all Gaussian entries are enumerated exactly.
